Industrialist

Roblox Game Review & Guide

About The Game

Industrialist is a game made on March 25th 2022 by Mamytema Studios, themed around factory building and industry expansion while managing the polluting environment. Heavily inspired by Mindustry, Factorio & some other industry games.


Platform: Roblox

Genre: construction and management simulation game

Key Game Features

Pollution management

Keep track of your pollution level and keep it under control.

Production lines

Plan, optimize, and upgrade your manufacturing systems.

Economic strategy

Manage money and research new technology.

Immersive mechanics

Engage with immersive and modular machine systems.

How To Play

When you start playing the game, you will be introduced to the tutorial, making the basic coal factory your first factory.

After the player finishes their first factory, they will be introduced to the research system, which will allow them to build more advanced factories.

Finishing the tutorial will unlock every tool for the player to explore and learn how each of the tools works. At this point in the game, players will need more power to support their power and will need to make better power source.

When you get enough money, power, and research, you will need to expand your production line to make better items and more items for more money and research.

If you build your factory unoptimized, you will eventually run out of space to expand, so you will need to start optimizing to maximize money and power within the limited space.
